How to Use

This section specifies the contents of this app and their features.


New Game

Here you can begin setting up a game. Select singles or doubles and write the players' names, the game will immediately start.

The player who's name is highlighted in white is the one that should be currently serving. The player one will always be the first one serving at the start of the match.

Players

A list of registered players and their profile pictures. Tapping on each player will display that player's win/lose record.

Matches

A list of previously finished matches. Singles and doubles are divided in two different lists detailing the result of each match and the players involved in it.

Extras

Here you get access to this section detailing the app's features and the rules for table tennis. You can also choose to restart all of the data currently stored.

Main Rules

  • In singles, the center lines serve no purpose, so ignore them.
  • In doubles, the ball shall touch successively the right half court of server and receiver.
  • The ball must rest on an open hand palm. Then it must be tossed up and struck so the ball first bounces on the server's side and then the opponent's side.
  • For each game, the first player to reach 11 points wins that game, however a game must be won by at least a two point margin.
  • A point is scored if an opponent's free hand touches the playing surface.
  • The edges of the table are part of the legal table surface, but not the sides.
